STARTING BUSINESS

Comparing the process of starting a business inChina and U.S.
So you have a good business idea, but how do you turn it into a business before others do? The process of formally registering a company offer in the world's two economies: U.S. and China. This project aims to compare the processes and shed light on bureaucratic obstacles and high costs that deter entrepreneurship.
